THE FAMILY DIVISION

OTF (Only The Family) is the strategic development and community division of Blood Esports. While 4KT represents our elite competitive force, OTF focuses on building the foundation for future champions through skill development, team building, and strategic innovation.

We're more than just a clan - we're a family dedicated to nurturing talent, fostering teamwork, and creating opportunities for players to grow. Our focus is on long-term development, community building, and preparing players for the highest levels of competition.

OTF has established itself as a tournament powerhouse, consistently performing in African Battle Royale Community events and Blood Esports Rankings competitions.
