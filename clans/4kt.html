THE BEAUTY OF OUR SYMBOL

The Chinese characters in our clan tag 4零丨 carry deep meaning that reflects our philosophy and identity.

零 (Líng) - ZERO / PERFECTION

Represents the beginning, potential, and infinite possibilities. In gaming, it symbolizes starting fresh, endless growth, and the pursuit of perfect execution. Zero is not empty - it's full of potential waiting to be realized.

| (Gǔn) - VERTICAL LINE / PILLAR

Symbolizes strength, stability, and unwavering focus. It represents the unbreakable connection between skill and success, the straight path to victory, and the foundation upon which champions are built.

TOGETHER: 4零丨

The combination creates a powerful statement: "Four pillars of infinite potential." It represents our four core values: Skill, Strategy, Unity, and Excellence. The tag reminds us that from zero (beginnings) comes greatness, supported by unwavering pillars of strength.
